Pulled Pork

Description

If you want the best pulled pork in all of Asgard, this is it! No being came up with a better one yet!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of dry barbecue rub (or more if you need more)
  • 1 (3 1/2) pound bone-in pork shoulder blade roast
  • 1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring, divided
  • 1 cup water, divided
  • 3/4 cup barbeque sauce (or more if you need)
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 12 soft white buns
  • 3/4 cup barbeque sasuce, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 210 degrees F (100 degrees C)
  2. Sprinkle dry rub generously on all sides of pork roast and place meat into a heavy pan or Dutch oven.
  3. Pour 1/4 of liquid smoke flavoring into each of two 6-ounce ramekins; fill ramekins with 1/2 cup water each. Place ramekins into the Dutch oven on either side of the roast. Place lid onto Dutch oven.
  4. Roast pork in the preheated oven until very tender, 12 hours. Remove roast from Dutch oven, place onto a work surface (such as a cutting board), and separate the meat from the bone using your fingers. Discard any pieces of large fat (or eat!)
  5. Roughly chop pork with a large knife or cleaver; drizzle with 3/4 cup barbeque sauce. Season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Spread about 1 tablespoon of barbeque sauce onto each bun and pile pork on buns to serve.
